SLEAP.ai

SLEAP is an open source deep-learning based framework for multi-animal pose tracking. It can be used to track any type or number of animals and includes an advanced labeling/training GUI for active learning and proofreading.

BOE-lympics

As part of ECE 110 - Fundamentals of Electrical and Computer Engineering — my group designed a Boe-Bot that can discern between a magic 8-ball, a rubber ducky, and a wooden pencil placed in the center of a circular ring and communicated the value corresponding to the object detected to other bots, then finally executed a celebration depending on the value sent by a separate group.

Noah's Ark

Flash floods pose a threat to both people and their property and without a proper detection system individuals are unable to react quickly to avoid any extensive damage or harm. We were tasked with designing a system that accurately measures the depth of a body of water and consistently transmits this data to the user, warning them of any imminent flooding.
